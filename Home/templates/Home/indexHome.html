IntelliWell

An optimization tool for oil and gas wells.

Inflow Models (IPRs)

IntelliWell has possible set of Inflow models covering vertical, deviated, horizontal, multilayer and multilateral geometries. Furthermore, novel development has seen the realisation of unique inflow models that account for changing PVT conditions in the well drainage area as well as in multiple zones. This allows optimization studies,analysis of skin, the application of sand control measures and many other sensitivities to be conducted easily.
